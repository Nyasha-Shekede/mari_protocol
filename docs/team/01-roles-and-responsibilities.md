## Conflict Resolution

### Process:
1. **Direct conversation** (try to resolve 1-on-1)
2. **Mediation** (third founder mediates)
3. **Advisor input** (if needed)
4. **Vote** (majority wins, but aim for consensus)

### Principles:
- Assume good intent
- Focus on what's best for Mari
- Data over opinions
- Speed over perfection

## Exit Scenarios

### If Someone Wants to Leave:
- Unvested equity returns to company
- Vested equity can be bought back (fair market value)
- Non-compete for 12 months
- IP remains with company

### If Company is Acquired:
- Equity converts to acquisition price
- Founders may have retention bonuses
- Team decides together (majority vote)

### If Company Fails:
- Wind down responsibly
- Pay back investors (if possible)
- IP returns to founders
- Learn and move on
