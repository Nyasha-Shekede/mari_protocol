- Merchants can't afford card terminals

## SLIDE 5: SOLUTION

**Physics-secured payments that work online or offline:**

**Innovation 1: Physics Seal**
- User shakes phone to authorize payment
- Motion + GPS + timestamp = unique signature
- Cannot be faked remotely (laws of physics)
- 99.95% fraud detection accuracy

**Innovation 2: Offline Payments**
- Works via SMS when internet fails
- Detects offline mode automatically
- Perfect for load shedding and rural areas

**Result:**
- Instant (2-3 seconds vs 1-3 days)
- Cheap (R0.50 vs R5)
- Secure (99.95% fraud prevention)
- Universal (works everywhere)
